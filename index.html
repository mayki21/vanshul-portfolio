Background

Brief Profile

Vanshul Pali is a distinguished Advocate with over six years of extensive experience in arbitration, civil, commercial, constitutional, and regulatory litigation. Recognized for his strategic advocacy and result-oriented approach, he regularly represents clients before the Supreme Court of India, High Courts, Tribunals, and various judicial and quasi-judicial forums across the country.

Mr. Pali presently serves as Panel Counsel for Central Government Litigation before the Supreme Court of India, Empanelled Advocate representing the Union of India before the Delhi High Court, Panel Counsel for the Municipal Corporation of Delhi (MCD), and Panel Counsel for the Delhi State Legal Services Authority (DLSA). These appointments reflect the trust reposed in him by public institutions and his commitment to advancing the rule of law and access to justice.

His practice spans a broad spectrum of contentious and advisory matters, including commercial disputes, corporate litigation, insolvency proceedings, white-collar crime investigations, fraud and corruption matters, regulatory enforcement actions, public law disputes, and domestic and international arbitration. He regularly appears before the NCLT, NCLAT, NCDRC, Appellate Tribunal for Forfeited Property (ATFP), High Courts, District Courts, and specialized tribunals across India.

Mr. Pali is particularly sought after for his expertise in high-stakes arbitration and complex commercial disputes, where he advises corporations, public sector entities, entrepreneurs, and high-net-worth individuals. He frequently assists retired judges, senior advocates, government bodies, and political organizations in domestic and international arbitral proceedings, including strategy formulation, pleadings, award drafting, hearing preparation, evidence management, and enforcement proceedings.

With a strong presence across major legal and commercial hubs, he operates through offices in New Delhi, Bengaluru, and Goa, enabling seamless representation and legal support across jurisdictions. His practice is built on a foundation of legal excellence, meticulous preparation, commercial awareness, and an unwavering commitment to safeguarding his clients' interests.

Through a combination of courtroom advocacy, strategic counsel, and sector-specific expertise, Vanshul Pali continues to provide comprehensive legal solutions to individuals, businesses, institutions, and government bodies navigating complex legal challenges.
